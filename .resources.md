# Resources

These are the websites/repositories/anything else that I used that helped me understand how to best format my code, files, and also taught me more about ARM and the Raspberry Pi. The resources are listed in random order. Some I have spent days, maybe weeks on, and others I probably only looked at for a couple of minutes. It is important to me that I keep them all documented here, in case I ever want to go back to one.
